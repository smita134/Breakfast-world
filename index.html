Breakfast Around the World

Tasty morning traditions from across the globe!

Japan

Dish: Grilled fish, miso soup, rice, pickles

Try it: Pair miso soup with steamed rice and tamagoyaki (egg roll)!

France

Dish: Croissant, butter, jam, and coffee

Try it: Warm up a flaky croissant and dip it in hot chocolate.

Mexico

Dish: Chilaquiles with salsa, cheese, eggs

Try it: Top tortilla chips with red salsa and a fried egg!
